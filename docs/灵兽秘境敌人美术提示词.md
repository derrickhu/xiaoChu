# 灵兽秘境敌人 — AI 生图提示词 v4

> 每只宠物生成 **1 张图**（1:1 方图，2×2 四宫格），上行=普通形态，下行=觉醒形态，左列=头像，右列=全身，后续剪裁拆分为 4 张。
> 画风：可爱水墨国风 + 柔光体积感 Q 版，面向女性玩家审美优化

---

## 一、画风定位

**核心风格**：中国水墨淡彩 × 可爱 Q 版 × 柔光体积感

不同于严格的传统水墨，本批资源追求**可爱、精致、有吸引力**的画面：
- **比例**：Q 版大头小身（头身比 3:1），圆润的轮廓和线条，极致的萌感
- **线条**：毛笔书法式墨线描边，有粗细变化但整体流畅柔和
- **上色**：水墨晕染底色 + 柔和的赛璐珞明暗层次，强调**体积感和立体感**——用浅色高光和深色阴影表现圆润质感
- **眼睛**：大而圆的灵动眼睛，有星光/宝石般的高光反射，不同宠物可以有不同颜色的瞳色（不局限于属性色）
- **配色**：**大胆混搭**——允许属性色为主调但加入对比色、莫兰迪色、马卡龙色作为点缀，拒绝单一死板配色
- **质感**：毛发蓬松柔软、鳞片闪烁、羽毛轻盈、甲壳光泽——通过细微的笔触变化体现
- **饰品**：**每只宠物必须有独特的配饰**（丝带、铃铛、花环、围巾、头冠、耳环、护腕、斗篷等），不允许所有宠物都戴同样的"珠子项链"
- **美学**：中国仙侠神话可爱化，温暖亲切，像是可以收养的神兽宝宝

**普通形态 vs 觉醒形态**：
- 普通形态：可爱清新，配饰简约，体现幼态和萌感
- 觉醒形态：**必须非常精致**——华丽的金色/宝石点缀、更复杂的花纹和装饰、流光溢彩的效果暗示（通过色彩渐变而非实际发光）、更自信的表情、更华丽的饰品升级

**抠图约束**：
- 清晰的黑色墨线描边轮廓（毛笔风格，有粗细变化）
- 描边外侧不得有白色描边/光晕/颜色溢出
- 黑色墨线即为最外层边界
- 禁止模糊、辉光、半透明羽化

| 项目 | 规格 |
|------|------|
| 宽高比 | **1:1 方图** |
| 布局 | 2×2 四宫格：上行=普通形态，下行=觉醒形态；左列=头像半身像，右列=全身战斗像 |
| 背景 | 纯浅灰色 `#E0E0E0`，覆盖整张画布 |

## 三、通用提示词模板（四宫格合并版）

```
A single 1:1 square image divided into a 2×2 grid of 4 equal panels on one canvas, solid plain single-color background light gray #E0E0E0 across the entire image. All 4 panels depict the EXACT SAME creature species with IDENTICAL base body colors, fur/scale patterns, eye color, face shape, and art style — the only difference between normal (top) and awakened (bottom) is the accessories.

TOP-LEFT PANEL — NORMAL FORM AVATAR: half-body bust portrait from head to mid-torso, adorable chibi creature, big head small body ratio 3:1, showing head, neck, chest, shoulders as one connected figure — not a floating head. Centered with clear margin from all edges especially the top. The face retains full animal anatomy: protruding animal snout/muzzle, small cute animal nose, animal mouth, animal proportions — NOT a flat human face. Large sparkling animal eyes with star-shaped highlights. EXPRESSION: warm, happy, cute, charming — a joyful or curious smile, NOT sad, NOT pouting. This is the normal base form — young, cute, simple charming accessories.

TOP-RIGHT PANEL — NORMAL FORM FULL BODY: the exact same normal creature in full body view. MUST be FRONT-FACING toward the viewer — NO sideways, NO profile. Eyes looking DIRECTLY at the viewer/camera. Creative battle-ready stance, fills 70-80% of panel height — pose can be varied (leap, charge, defend, etc.) but always face camera.

BOTTOM-LEFT PANEL — AWAKENED FORM AVATAR: the SAME creature species as top row. Awakened form can include: (1) upgraded accessories with gold trim and jewels, (2) more refined/richer body colors, (3) body form evolution (e.g. 1 tail → multiple tails, wings expanded, horns grown). Same half-body bust framing. EXPRESSION: same cute charming expression — warm, happy, adorable.

BOTTOM-RIGHT PANEL — AWAKENED FORM FULL BODY: the awakened creature in full body. MUST be FRONT-FACING — NO sideways, NO profile. Eyes looking at viewer. Use a DIFFERENT action/pose from top-right — if normal form leaps, awakened may charge or vice versa; creative variety. Awakened pose should feel more powerful or evolved.

CRITICAL: Normal and awakened full body (right column) must have DIFFERENT poses/actions. Awakened form (bottom row) adds accessories + can have refined body colors + body form evolution (e.g. more tails). Same species, same face and eyes. Both full-body panels: face camera, eyes at viewer, never sideways.

UNIFIED STYLE FOR ALL 4 PANELS: Chinese ink wash painting inspired with soft cel-shading for volume and depth, gentle highlights on rounded surfaces for 3D feel, calligraphy brush-style black ink outlines with varying thickness, rich vibrant colors mixing traditional Chinese pigments with pastel accent tones, fluffy soft texture rendering for fur and fabric, delicate detail work on accessories and patterns, warm inviting atmosphere, Chinese Xianxia mythology aesthetic with kawaii charm, NOT Western fantasy, NOT photorealistic, NOT 3D rendered, clean sharp ink-line edges for easy cutout.

STRICTLY FORBIDDEN: any text, letters, words, writing, watermark, symbols, seals, labels, captions. STRICTLY FORBIDDEN: white outline outside black ink outlines, white border, white edge glow, white halo. Also forbidden: glow effects, blur, soft edges, lens flare. STRICTLY FORBIDDEN: humanoid form, human face, human body, flat human-like face on an animal. STRICTLY FORBIDDEN: any part of the character extending beyond or touching any panel boundary — every character must be fully contained within its panel.

NORMAL FORM DESCRIPTION: {普通描述}
AWAKENED FORM UPGRADES: {觉醒描述}
```
