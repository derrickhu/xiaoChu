# 开场漫画 AI 生图提示词

## 通用风格说明

- **风格**: 可爱东方奇幻水彩风（Cute Eastern Fantasy Watercolor），柔和线条，色彩鲜明
- **主角**: 中性小童，圆脸大眼，蓬松短发，穿白金色道袍，整体造型偏可爱偏女性化
- **比例**: 竖屏 9:16（适配手机全屏，推荐分辨率 750x1334 或更高）
- **品质**: 手游启动画面品质，高完成度插画
- **一致性**: 两张图必须保持风格、人物造型、色调统一

```
A vertical portrait illustration in cute Eastern fantasy watercolor style. A small androgynous child (gender-neutral, round face, big eyes, short fluffy hair, wearing a simple white-and-gold Daoist robe) is falling through swirling clouds toward a mystical mountain landscape below. ## 第 2 页 —「灵宠降临」
```

### 文件

`assets/intro/intro_2.jpg`（已生成，498KB JPG）

### 英文 Prompt

```
A vertical portrait illustration in cute Eastern fantasy watercolor style. A small androgynous child (same character as page 1: gender-neutral, round face, big eyes, short fluffy hair, white-and-gold Daoist robe) stands at the edge of a cliff, looking scared. A menacing dark beast with glowing red eyes and sharp fangs lunges toward the child from the shadows. At the critical moment, five brilliant beams of light descend from the sky in five colors: gold (metal), green (wood), brown/amber (earth), blue (water), and red (fire). Each beam materializes into a cute but fierce spirit pet creature, surrounding and protecting the child. The spirit pets glow with elemental energy, forming a protective circle. Strong light-dark contrast: the spirit pet side is warm and radiant with five-colored light, the beast side is cold and shadowy. The child's expression changes from fear to wonder. Cute art style, vibrant colors, dynamic composition. Mobile game splash screen quality, 9:16 aspect ratio.
```

### 中文描述

可爱东方奇幻水彩风格，竖构图。同一个中性小童（圆脸大眼、蓬松短发、白金色道袍）站在悬崖边，表情害怕。一只凶恶的暗色妖兽（红色发光眼睛、尖牙）从阴影中扑来。就在危急时刻，五道不同颜色的光芒（金色/绿色/棕琥珀色/蓝色/红色，对应金木土水火五行）从天而降，每道光化作一只可爱但威武的灵宠，环绕守护小童。灵宠们散发五行元素之力，形成保护圈。画面有强烈的光暗对比——灵宠一侧明亮温暖、五彩斑斓，妖兽一侧阴暗冰冷。小童的表情从恐惧转为惊叹。画风可爱，色彩鲜艳，构图动感。手游启动画面品质，9:16 竖屏比例。

### 配文

- "一头凶兽挡住了小修士的去路"
- "危急之中，五道灵光从天而降——"
- "金、木、水、火、土，五行灵宠降临守护！"
